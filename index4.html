Royal Nupe Heritage

The Masaba Royal Lineage

Etsu Nupe Dynasty of Bida

The Shaffi family traces its roots to the prestigious Masaba Royal Family of Bida, established by the legendary Masaba dan Malam Dendo in the early 19th century. This royal lineage carries the esteemed title of Etsu Nupe, signifying leadership over the Nupe people.


Alhaji Muhammadu Mustapha, known as Barape Nupe, was a revered patriarch of this royal lineage. His heritage represents centuries of Nupe authority, cultural influence, and leadership that continues through Governor Umar Bago.


The Bida Emirate remains the cultural heart of the Nupe people, with traditions passed down through generations of the Masaba dynasty.
